# Employee Handbook (Demo)

## Annual Leave Policy

Employees who have completed 12 months of service are eligible for annual leave.
The default annual leave allowance is 5 working days per year.

## Reimbursement Policy

The reimbursement process includes the following steps:
1. Fill in the reimbursement form.
2. Attach required receipts.
3. Submit for manager approval.
4. Finance will review and process payment.

Reimbursements are typically processed within 5 business days after approval.

## Probation Period

The probation period is 3 months unless otherwise stated in the employment contract.
